Recipe for Peanut Butter & Syrup
	Ingredients:
		2 tbsp of peanut butter
		1/2 cup of syrup

	Process
		1. Put 2 tbsp of peanut butter into a bowl
		2. Poor in 1/2 cup of syrup
		3. Stir till you get the consistency you want
		4. Enjoy!

	This goes well with toast (dip the toast into the mix) or
	saltines/crackers.
